VR SIDDHARTHA ENGINEERING COLLEGE



Velagapudi Ramakrishna Siddhartha Engineering College Vijayawada Andhra Pradesh is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education and was established in 1977. It is one of the pioneer Engineering Institutes in the state of Andra Pradesh. VRSEC is an autonomous college affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University [JNTUK], Kakinada. It is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation [NBA], certified under ISO and approved by All India Council of Technical Education [AICTE]. It is also accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council as a NAAC-A grade Institute. The Institute is situated in 24.05 acres of land.

Velagapudi Ramakrishna Siddhartha Engineering College was established in 1977 as the first private self financing engineering college in the state of composite Andhra Pradesh and is sponsored by “Siddhartha Academy of General and technical Education”, a registered society run by philanthropists from different walks of life. The college is in autonomous status granted by UGC since 2006. The college has the distinction of being the first technical institution in Andhra Pradesh to secure extension of autonomous status without physical visit for a decade at a stretch up to 2027-2028. The college is offering B.Tech. programme in 9 branches with an intake of 1260, Seven M.Tech. programs with 90, in addition to 60 each in MBA and MCA programmes. At present, the college has 315 well-experienced faculty members including 149 members with PhD qualification. The college is spread over 24.05 acres of land with a built-up area of 66,165 sq.m, meeting all the norms of AICTE and is permanently affiliated to JNTUK,Kakinada.
